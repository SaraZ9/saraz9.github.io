Research Assistant at UBC

Sara Zhang

University of British Columbia Undergraduate Research Opportunities
Location: Vancouver, BC
Dates: Oct. 2018 – Mar. 2019

As a Research Assistant, I focused on quantifying structural stigma by analyzing the implementation of social and legal protections for sexual and gender minorities in North America. I presented my findings at the Multidisciplinary Undergraduate Research Conference, contributing to a broader understanding of the topic and supporting ongoing research efforts.

Key Contributions:

  • Quantified structural stigma levels through historical data analysis
  • Presented research findings at a multidisciplinary conference
  • Contributed to UBC’s understanding of social protections for marginalized groups
